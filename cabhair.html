Search syntax: Boolean operators. AGUS, NO, GAN. By default, AGUS. Must use uppercase with these. "foo bar" "bar foo" "foo AND bar" Simple GAN example. Simple NO example. Parentheses can be used to group searches "GAN (foo AND bar)" No wildcards. To search for a phrase, use double quotes, e.g. "Kevin Scannell" "clapsholas Ceilteach"
